Getting Started...

  • Get Link/ Invitation code.
  • Register Account.
  • Check out the Machine list.
  • Go to Wallet.
  • Do the Recharge for the desired Machine.
  • Choose from the various Top-Up channels.
  • Enter the amount and pay using the deired payment option.
  • After confirmation, Go back to the Wallet.
  • Check for the recharged amount that shows on your Recharge Wallet.
  • Go to the Machine page.
  • Make the purchase of the Machine.
  • Confirm the purchase by going to:
     Personal > My Device
     you can see the purchased device in there.
  • Now, you can recommend this KNC thing to others and earn from referals.
